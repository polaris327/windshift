What is a parametric trigger?
A parametric trigger is an index based type of insurance that is “triggered” when the index surpasses a predefined value.
What is the index?
The index varies depending on the type of insurance being sought. In WindShift’s case, it may include localized wind speed calculations, wind speed footprints, or the size of the event and distance from the track, or all three!
How do these triggers get measured?
Anemometers are instruments that measure the speed of wind, currents, or gases.
