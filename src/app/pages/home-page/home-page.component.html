What is WindShift?
WindShift is an emerging type of supplemental windstorm insurance that uses “parametric triggers” for losses, creating a transparent payout process significantly quicker than traditional insurance.
If a loss event occurs in your area, you will get paid promptly.
We promise, no catch.
As long as triggers are met, it doesn’t matter what caused the losses – You will be paid your pre-defined sum!
